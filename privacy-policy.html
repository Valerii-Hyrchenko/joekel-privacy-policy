SCOPE

This policy applies to the Android version of the App distributed via Google Play.

DATA WE COLLECT

A) Without an online account (tutorial, play vs bot, local play on one device, nearby Bluetooth/Wi‑Fi multiplayer):

  • Preferences stored on your device (sound settings, rules, language, tutorial progress).
  • For nearby multiplayer: a display name you choose and connection data exchanged directly between devices. This is not sent to our servers.
  • Consent record on your device: when you accept this policy, we store on your device the time of acceptance, policy version, device model, OS version, App version, language, and an Android installation identifier (to document consent).
  • Anonymous usage data (Google Analytics for Firebase): after you accept, we collect anonymous information about how the App is used (e.g., which screens are opened and which game modes are started) to improve the App and fix bugs. This is not linked to your online account unless you sign in.

B) With an online account (email or Google sign-in):

  • Account data: email address, username/nickname, unique user ID (UID), and sign-in method.
  • Profile: username and UID are visible to other players; email and friends list are private.
  • When you create or sign in to an online account, we also store your consent record on our servers (same information as in section 3(A)).
  • Online play: game states, moves, match results, ratings, leaderboard, invitations, and online/offline status.
  • Push token and activity timestamp: if you allow notifications, we store a push token and a timestamp of your last activity to send game-invite alerts and manage inactive devices (disable anytime in device settings).
  • Language preference linked to your account.

C) Through our hosting when you use online features:

  • Technical and security-related data processed by our providers.

We do not use advertising SDKs, sell your personal data, or track you across other apps or websites for advertising. Because we do not track you across third-party websites or apps, the App does not respond to "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals.
